About Western Astrology

Western astrology is based on the tropical zodiac, divided into twelve signs from Aries to Pisces. Each sign reflects a core style of expression, motivation, and temperament.

Chinese Bazi / Ziwei

Foundational placeholders for a future detailed Bazi & Ziwei system.

Bazi Overview

Bazi (Four Pillars of Destiny) interprets your life path using year, month, day, and hour of birth, mapping them to Heavenly Stems and Earthly Branches.

Ziwei Doushu Overview

Ziwei Doushu charts positions of key stars in life palaces to describe personality, career, relationships, and fate.

Palmistry & Face Reading

High-level guides to interpreting physical features symbolically.

Palmistry Basics

  • Life line: vitality and general life energy.
  • Heart line: emotional style and relationships.
  • Head line: thinking style and communication.

Face Reading Basics

  • Forehead: early life, thinking, and planning.
  • Eyes: spirit, emotional expression, intuition.
  • Mouth & jaw: willpower, persistence, material life.
